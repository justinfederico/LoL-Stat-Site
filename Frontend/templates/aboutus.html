ABOUT US

JAREL MACANAS

> FRONTEND DESIGNER <

I am currently a fourth-year Computer Engineering Student at San Diego State University. This semester kinda got me wrecked, but I graduate after this so all is well.

For this project, my job was mostly working on the frontend development of our website, additionally helping out with the backend and API when possible.

Much of the work I've done has gone into creating the base page of our website as well as the several containers that display much of the data taken from RIOT GAMES' API. Bootstrap is heavily utilized to create a majority of the frontend design while Javascript was added to offer some simple animations.

Basically, I made the website look as appealing to our users as possible. I'm no artist, but maybe I should've been.

I am currently a fourth-year Computer Engineering Student at San Diego State University. For my part in the website development, I focused much more on backend operations.
